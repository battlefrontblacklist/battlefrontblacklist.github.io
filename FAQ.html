What is the purpose of a blacklist?

People can be blacklisted for a variety of reasons that ultimately depend on the nature of the relationship between the organization compiling the blacklist and the blacklisted entity. Regardless of the specific reason for blacklisting, the ultimate purpose is to restrict future interactions between the blacklisted individual and the organization.

What is the purpose of a whitelist?

People can be whitelisted for a variety of reasons that ultimately depend on the nature of the relationship between the organization compiling the whitelist and the whitelisted entity. Regardless of the specific reason for whitelisting, the ultimate purpose is to maintain future interactions between the whitelisted individual and the organization.
